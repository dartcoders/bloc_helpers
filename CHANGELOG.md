## 1.0.0

* Initial release.
* `BlocEffectMixin` and `CubitEffectMixin` for one-time side effects.
* `BlocEffectListener` widget for consuming effects in the UI.
* `BlocTransformers` with `droppable`, `restartable`, and `debounce` event transformers.
* `AppCubitHandler` base class with lifecycle hooks and safe emit.
